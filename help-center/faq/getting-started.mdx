## Quick Start

Creating your first doc page is simple:

1. Log in to your * dashboard.
2. Click "New Page" in the sidebar.
3. Provide a page title and optional description.
4. Use the editor to add content (text, code, etc).
5. Click "Save" to publish your page.

You can edit, delete, or move pages anytime from the dashboard menu.
