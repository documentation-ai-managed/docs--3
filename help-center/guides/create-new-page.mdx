---
title: Creating a New Documentation Page
description: Step-by-step guide to add a page in *.
---

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your project">
    Log in and select your documentation project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Page'">
    Find the "New Page" button in the sidebar and click it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add page content">
    Enter a title and start writing your content with the rich editor.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and view">
    Click "Save". Your new page will appear in the navigation.
  </Step>
</Steps>
